Biography

Arctic Monkeys are a British rock band formed in 2002 in Sheffield. The band is made up of Alex Turner (vocalist and guitarist), Matt Helders (drummer), Jamie Cook (guitarist) and Nick O'Malley (bassist). They are widely recognized as one of the greatest British bands of the 21st century.

The band is also known for their popular singles such as "I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor", "Fluorescent Adolescent", and "Do I Wanna Know?". Arctic Monkeys continue to be an influential force on the global music scene.
